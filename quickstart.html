Quickstart


This page will walk you through the basics of using your uCritAir.
Many passages here will reference the device's buttons by name. Please refer to the hardware page for the button layout.
Not every feature of the uCritAir is covered in detail here. The rest of the documentation may come in handy if you want to know more about a particular feature.

Startup

Press START to wake the device from sleep. If the device is in protected-off mode, insert a paperclip into the reset hole until the reset button is depressed to wake the device. It will take a few seconds for the device to wake, especially from protected-off. Upon waking, the device will refresh its e-ink display. This too will take a few seconds.

The Apartment

Upon waking the uCritAir you will find yourself in the apartment screen. The apartment screen is roughly divided into three sections:

The top section contains the window, the arcade, and the vending machine. The uCritAir's display has touch capability, and all three of these props can be used via a touch. Tapping the window opens the dashboard, Tapping the arcade lists available arcade games, and tapping the vending machine opens the shop where you can buy and sell items.

The middle section contains the bulk of the apartment's floor space. Your critter spends most of its time here -- try petting it from the touch screen while it walks about. So that you and your critter feel at home, this space can be decorated to your heart's content.

The bottom section contains the screen buttons which are used to access various features.
The five screen buttons, from left to right:
  • Launch the feeding minigame
  • Launch the fishing minigame
  • List available toys
  • Enter decoration mode
  • Open the menu

Using The Menu

Navigate menu items using the D-pad. Press A to engage with the hovered menu item, or press B retreat to the previous menu screen. All features are directly or indirectly accessible through the menu. Customization options are also exposed through the menu. Below are some menu paths that may be of interest to you:
  • MENU / INSIGHTS : View information about your critter and its health
  • MENU / INVENTORY : View all of the items in your inventory
  • MENU / VENDING MACHINE : Open the shop
  • MENU / ARCADE : Play arcade games
  • MENU / EXPLORE : Explore the overworld
  • MENU / DASHBOARD : Open the air quality dashboard
  • MENU / SETTINGS : Configure the device
  • MENU / SETTINGS / SYSTEM / SET CLOCK + LOG RATE : Set the device's date, time, and the rate at which it logs air quality samples
  • MENU / SETTINGS / GAMEPLAY / DASHBOARD FIRST : Tell the device to boot into the dashboard rather than the apartment
  • MENU / SETTINGS / GAMEPLAY / PAUSE CRITTER CARE : Pause time for your critter, preventing it from draining its stats, taking lifetime damage, and growing old
  • MENU / POWER : Put the device to sleep or shut it down

Getting Items

Many features of the uCritAir require the use of items. Feeding the critter requires food, decorating the apartment requires decorations, crafting requires ingredients, etc. Many items can be purchased from the shop using coins.

To collect coins, sell items at the shop or play arcade games which offer coins as a reward. The subset of items which can be sold is relatively small. The most reliable way to make money is to sell fish that you've caught and crafting materials that you've collected.

Caring For Your Critter

Your critter has a lifespan and 3 core stats: vigour, focus, and spirit. The starting lifespan is always 30 days, but failure to mitigate air quality crises can reduce your critter's lifespan. Additionally, if you let any of your critter's core 3 stats reach 0, its lifespan will shrink by 1 day for every day that you let its stats remain critically low.

Preventing stat-induced lifespan damage is relatively easy. Feeding your critter raises its vigour, taking your critter fishing raises its focus, and playing with your critter raises its spirit. If you are exposed to critically bad air quality repeatedly for too long, some amount of crisis-induced lifespan damage is inevitable. However, items likes masks, air filters, and UV lamps can be purchased and used to automatically mitigate some crises.

Always remember, though, that the best way to keep your critter out of harm's way is simply to keep yourself out of harm's way too. Breathe good air, and so will your critter. If any aspect of critter care becomes unmanageable, you can always try pausing it the from the screen found under MENU / SETTINGS / GAMEPLAY.

Exploring The Overworld

Navigating to MENU / EXPLORE will transport you to the overworld. In the overworld, use the D-pad to move, A to interact, and B to sprint. There are some NPCs to talk to, locations to interact with, and resources to collect. However, the overworld is still heavily under construction, so there is not very much to do there yet! To return to the apartment from the overworld, navigate to MENU / GO HOME.
