Arcade


The arcade offers minigames to be played for enjoyment and the occasional reward.The current games are Snack, Sweep, and Foursquares

Snack

Maneuver Snackcat with the D-pad to snap up snacks and coins.Beware! If you collide with the walls or yourself, it's game over.

Sweep

Carefully unearth a minefield tile-by-tile.Tearing up a mine spells certain doom. The number on a tile indicates its distance in tiles from the nearest mine.

Foursquares

Stack up 4-squared geometry to form a continuous block.A fully-formed block will dissolve and score you points.If your geometry touches the ceiling, it's game over.
