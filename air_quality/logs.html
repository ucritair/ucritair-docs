Logs


Air quality data is logged at a user-configurable rate. A higher logging rate will fill the uCritAir's storage space more quickly and also consume more battery. Logs are intended to persist indefinitely until cleared. If logs are cleared, dashboard features that rely on logs will be unavailable until a handful of new logs are made.

The logged metrics are:
  • CO2
  • PM 2.5
  • PN 10.0
  • NOx Index
  • VOC Index
  • Temperature
  • Relative Humidity
  • Pressure
